DemoX

This brief course is designed to show new students how to take a course on edX. You will learn how to navigate the edX platform and complete your first course! From there, we will help you get started choosing the course that best fits your interests, needs, and dreams. Have questions before taking the demo course? Check our student FAQs.

You will learn

How to navigate the edX platform How to connect with other edX learners in a course How to answer problems on the edX platform How grades work in edX courses How to complete your first course
